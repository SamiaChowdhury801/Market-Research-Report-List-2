<p><strong>The Automotive Charge Air Cooler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-Cooled Charge Air Cooler</li><li>Liquid-Cooled Charge Air Cooler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive charge air cooler market primarily consists of two types: air-cooled and liquid-cooled charge air coolers. Air-cooled charge air coolers use ambient air to dissipate heat from the compressed air, making them simpler and lighter, often preferred in applications where weight and cost are critical. In contrast, liquid-cooled charge air coolers utilize a coolant to absorb heat, offering more efficient cooling in higher-performance engines. Both types enhance engine efficiency and performance by lowering intake air temperatures.</p></p>
