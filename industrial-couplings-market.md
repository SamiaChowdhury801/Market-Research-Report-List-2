<p><ul><li>Flexible Couplings</li><li>Rigid Couplings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Couplings market is categorized into Flexible and Rigid Couplings. Flexible Couplings are designed to accommodate misalignment between connected shafts, absorbing shocks and vibrations, thus enhancing equipment lifespan. They are ideal for applications where dynamic changes occur. In contrast, Rigid Couplings provide a solid connection, offering precise alignment and high torque transmission but are less tolerant to misalignment. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, ensuring efficient power transmission and mechanical integrity in machinery and equipment.</p></p>
