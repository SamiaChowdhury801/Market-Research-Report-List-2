<p><ul><li>Disc Aeration Systems</li><li>Pipe Aeration Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aeration systems for wastewater treatment are essential for enhancing the biological breakdown of pollutants. Disc aeration systems utilize circular discs submerged in water, which rotate to create air bubbles, promoting oxygen transfer and mixing. Pipe aeration systems involve the installation of air diffusion pipes or nozzles that release fine air bubbles into the wastewater. Both types effectively increase dissolved oxygen levels, supporting aerobic microbial activity and ensuring efficient treatment processes in various wastewater treatment facilities.</p></p>
