<p><strong>The Portable Exhaust Gas Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NDIR Absorption Method</li><li>Electrochemical Method</li><li>Hydrogen Flame Ionization Method</li><li>Chemiluminescence Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable exhaust gas analyzer market includes several types based on detection methods. The NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) absorption method measures gas concentration by analyzing infrared light absorption, ideal for CO2 and hydrocarbons. The electrochemical method detects gases through chemical reactions, offering sensitivity for pollutants like CO. The hydrogen flame ionization method measures total hydrocarbons through ionization in a flame, known for high specificity. Lastly, the chemiluminescence method analyzes NOx emissions based on light produced in chemical reactions, providing accuracy for nitrogen oxides.</p></p>
