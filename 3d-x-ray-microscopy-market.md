<p><p>The 3D X-Ray Microscopy market encompasses three primary types: Contact Micro-radiography, Projection Micro-radiography, and X-ray Diffraction. Contact Micro-radiography involves placing samples directly against a film, yielding high-resolution images for detailed analysis. Projection Micro-radiography captures images from various angles, reconstructing 3D models for comprehensive internal examinations. X-ray Diffraction measures how X-rays interact with sample lattices, providing insights into material structures and properties. Together, these techniques enhance imaging capabilities across materials science, biology, and engineering sectors.</p></p>
