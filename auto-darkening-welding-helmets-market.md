<p><p>The welding helmet market features two main types: passive and auto-darkening welding helmets. Passive welding helmets have fixed lenses that provide constant filtration, requiring the user to flip them up or down when adjusting their light exposure. In contrast, auto-darkening welding helmets feature specialized filters that automatically adjust their tint in response to the brightness of the welding arc, enhancing convenience and visual comfort. This innovation allows welders to maintain visibility and protection without manually adjusting their gear during tasks.</p></p>
