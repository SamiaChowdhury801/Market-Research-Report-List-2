<p><p>The cable sensors market encompasses various types, primarily including NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) and PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) sensors. NTC sensors decrease resistance as temperature rises, making them ideal for precise temperature monitoring, while PTC sensors increase resistance with temperature, offering overcurrent protection and self-regulating heating applications. Additionally, there are other sensor types, such as thermocouples and fiber optic sensors, each catering to specific applications in industrial, automotive, and consumer electronics sectors, enhancing performance and safety.</p></p>
