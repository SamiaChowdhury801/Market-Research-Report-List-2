<p><p>The automatic palletising equipment market comprises two main types: mechanical and robotic. Mechanical palletisers use fixed systems with tools like clamps and blades for stacking products efficiently and quickly in high-volume environments. In contrast, robotic palletisers utilize programmable robotic arms that offer flexibility and adaptability for varied product sizes and configurations. This modern approach allows for quicker reconfiguration and greater precision, catering to changing production demands while enhancing operational efficiency across diverse industries.</p></p>
