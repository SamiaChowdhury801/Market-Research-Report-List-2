<p><p>Dryer bundles encompass various drying methods tailored for industrial applications. Tape drying involves spreading material on a moving belt for efficient moisture removal. Tumble drying, commonly used for textiles, relies on rotating drums to circulate hot air. Box drying employs a chamber to expose materials to heat and airflow, ensuring even drying. Lastly, drying towers utilize vertical structures where materials descend through heated air, facilitating rapid moisture extraction. Each method serves specific requirements in the drying process, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
