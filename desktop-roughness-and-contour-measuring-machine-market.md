<p><p>The desktop roughness and contour measuring machine market comprises two main types: contact and non-contact measuring machines. Contact roughness and contour measuring machines utilize tactile probes to physically touch the surface of the material, providing accurate surface texture measurements. In contrast, non-contact roughness and contour measuring machines employ optical or laser technology to assess surface characteristics without direct contact, reducing the risk of damage to sensitive samples. Both types cater to various industries requiring precise surface evaluation.</p></p>
