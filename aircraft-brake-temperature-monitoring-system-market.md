<p><strong>The Aircraft Brake Temperature Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-contact</li><li>Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Brake Temperature Monitoring System market is categorized into two primary types: non-contact and contact systems. Non-contact systems utilize infrared sensors to measure brake temperatures from a distance, offering quick and reliable readings without physical interaction. In contrast, contact systems involve direct temperature measurement through thermocouples or resistance temperature detectors integrated into the brake assembly. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safe aircraft operations by providing real-time data to prevent overheating and ensure optimal braking performance.</p></p>
