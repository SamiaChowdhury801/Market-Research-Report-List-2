<p><p>The train door lights market encompasses various types including ceiling lamps, wall lamps, and caudal lamps. Ceiling lamps are typically installed above the train doors, providing illumination for passengers boarding or exiting. Wall lamps are mounted on the sides of the train, enhancing visibility and safety along the entry points. Caudal lamps, designed for broader illumination, focus on illuminating the area behind the train, ensuring safety and preventing accidents during train operations in dimly lit environments. Each type plays a crucial role in passenger safety and comfort.</p></p>
