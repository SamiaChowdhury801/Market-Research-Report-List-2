<p><p>A Combined Cycle Steam Turbine (CCST) system integrates both gas and steam turbines to maximize efficiency and energy output. In this setup, the gas turbine generates electricity while the waste heat produced is utilized to generate steam, which then drives a steam turbine to produce additional power. This dual-cycle approach enhances overall efficiency, making CCST an attractive option for energy producers seeking to reduce fuel consumption and lower emissions.</p><p>The Combined Cycle Steam Turbine Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for cleaner energy sources and the need for efficient power generation technologies.
