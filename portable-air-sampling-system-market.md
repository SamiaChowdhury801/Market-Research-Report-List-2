<p><p>The Portable Air Sampling System market includes two primary types: dry collection and wet collection. Dry collection systems capture airborne particles using a filter or similar medium, ideal for analyzing dust and particulate matter in various environments. Wet collection systems, on the other hand, utilize liquid mediums to trap and analyze gaseous pollutants and aerosols, providing more comprehensive chemical analysis. Both types serve critical roles in environmental monitoring, industrial hygiene, and research, ensuring accurate air quality assessments and compliance.</p></p>
