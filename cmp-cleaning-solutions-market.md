<p><ul><li>Acidic</li><li>Basic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP Cleaning Solutions are categorized into acidic and basic market types based on their pH levels and application purposes. Acidic cleaning solutions typically contain compounds like citric or phosphoric acids, effective for removing metal oxides and residues from silicon wafers in semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, basic cleaning solutions use alkaline substances, such as sodium hydroxide, to dissolve organic contaminants and particles. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the cleanliness of substrates, enhancing the performance and yield of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
