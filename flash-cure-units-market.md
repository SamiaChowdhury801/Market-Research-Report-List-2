<p><p>Flash cure units are essential tools in the screen printing and textile industries, used for curing inks quickly to enhance production efficiency. The market is primarily segmented into two types: quartz and infrared. Quartz flash cure units utilize high-intensity quartz bulbs that emit concentrated heat, allowing for rapid curing of inks. In contrast, infrared flash cure units employ infrared radiation to heat the substrate evenly, providing a more gradual curing process. Each type caters to specific printing needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
