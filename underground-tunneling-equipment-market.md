<p><p>The underground tunneling equipment market primarily features two main types: Roadheaders and Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs). Roadheaders are versatile machines used for excavating rock in underground projects, offering flexibility in navigation and operation in varying geological conditions. In contrast, TBMs are large and highly specialized machines designed for efficient, circular tunneling, primarily employed in constructing deep tunnels for transportation systems. Both types play crucial roles in infrastructure development, each suited for specific tunneling applications and environments.</p></p>
