<p><p>The chemical composition analysis market encompasses various techniques used to identify and quantify substances in a sample. Chromatography separates components for analysis, while mass spectroscopy measures the mass-to-charge ratio of ions to determine molecular structures. Spectroscopy involves analyzing the interaction of light with matter to identify chemical compounds. Wet chemistry refers to traditional laboratory techniques that utilize liquid reagents for qualitative and quantitative analysis. Together, these methods provide comprehensive insights into the chemical makeup of various materials.</p></p>
