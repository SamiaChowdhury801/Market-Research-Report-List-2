<p><p>The vacuum coating equipment market includes several key types, such as evaporation and sputtering techniques. Evaporation type uses thermal energy to vaporize materials, allowing them to condense on a substrate, creating thin films. In contrast, sputtering type employs ionized gases to bombard a target material, ejecting particles onto the substrate. Other methods may include chemical vapor deposition or hybrid processes. Each technology offers distinct advantages for various applications, from optical coatings to electronic components.</p></p>
