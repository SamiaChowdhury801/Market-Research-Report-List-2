<p><p>The Completion Equipment market encompasses essential technologies for finalizing oil and gas wells. Casings are steel pipes that support well integrity, while cementing materials and equipment ensure the secure bond between casings and surrounding formations. Perforating guns create openings in the casing, allowing hydrocarbons to flow. Gravel packing involves placing gravel in the wellbore to prevent sand influx, enhancing production. Wellheads serve as the interface for surface facilities, controlling flow and pressure during extraction, thus ensuring operational safety and efficiency.</p></p>
