<p><p>The household food storage containers market is categorized into three main types: metal, glass, and plastic. Metal containers, often made of stainless steel, are durable and resistant to stains and odors but may lack transparency. Glass containers are favored for their non-reactive and microwave-safe properties, offering visibility and an eco-friendly option. Plastic containers are lightweight and versatile, featuring various designs and sizes, but can be less durable and may absorb odors over time. Each type serves unique consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
