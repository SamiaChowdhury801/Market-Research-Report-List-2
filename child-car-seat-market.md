<p><p>The child car seat market comprises three main types: infant car seats, convertible car seats, and booster seats. Infant car seats are designed for newborns and smaller infants, offering rear-facing installation for optimal safety. Convertible car seats can adjust from rear-facing to forward-facing as a child grows, accommodating a wider age range. Booster seats elevate older children to ensure proper seat belt positioning, providing safety until they reach the vehicle's standard seat height. Each type prioritizes child safety and comfort during travel.</p></p>
