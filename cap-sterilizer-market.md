<p><p>The cap sterilizer market comprises two primary types: horizontal and vertical sterilizers. Horizontal sterilizers operate with the sterilization chamber oriented horizontally, allowing for easy loading and unloading of caps, making them suitable for larger production lines. In contrast, vertical sterilizers have a vertical orientation, which is often space-saving and suitable for smaller batches. Both types ensure effective sterilization, but the choice between them depends on production requirements, space availability, and operational efficiency within the manufacturing process.</p></p>
