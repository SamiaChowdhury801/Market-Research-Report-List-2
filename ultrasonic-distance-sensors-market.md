<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic distance sensors are categorized into two main types: analog and digital. </p><p>Analog ultrasonic sensors provide continuous output proportional to the distance measured, making them suitable for applications requiring real-time distance data. They typically output voltage or current signals that vary with distance. </p><p>Digital ultrasonic sensors, on the other hand, deliver discrete distance measurements in digital format, often featuring integrated microcontrollers for processing. These sensors are ideal for applications needing precise measurements and easier integration with digital systems.</p></p>
